Welcome to SkaterShots NFT Collection

To get started with the SkaterShots NFT Collection, connect your MetaMask wallet with the SkaterShots application below.

SkaterShots NFT Collection, powered by the Seoplia Ethereum Testnet Blockchain, allows users to trade their favourite NHL trading cards in a fully digital, decentralized manner. SkaterShots mimics the act of opening trading card packs, owning sports trading cards, and selling your cards for varying values depending on their rarity.

Once connected to the SkaterShots Application, all users have the ability to:
  • Open a card pack to obtain a SkaterShots NFT for the small price of 0.001 Sepolia ETH.
  • View your SkaterShots NFT's, which include action shots of your favourite NHL stars.
  • Sell your SkaterShots NFT's for various prices depending on their rarity. NFT's representing successful teams from the 2023-24 NHL season have a higher sell price!
